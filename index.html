Bootstrap has its own responsive paddings and margins. wherein its classes, m stands for margin - mt ml mb mr. p stands for padding - pt pl pb pr
Bootstrap paddings and margins can be applied on specific sides
  • t - sets either margin or padding at the top of element: mt or pt
  • b - sets either margin or padding at the bottom side of our element: mb or pb
  • y - sets either margin or padding on y-axis, both top and bottom: my or py
  • x - sets either margin or padding on x-axis, both top and bottom: mx or px
  • l - sets either margin or padding on the left side: ml or pl
  • r - sets either margin or padding on the right side: mr or pr
  • blank - sets the margin or padding on all sides : m or p
